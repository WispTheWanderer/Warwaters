# Warwaters: a Command Line Rogue-Like Turn-Based Naval Combat Simulator

In *Warwaters*, you command a pirate fleet on a massive ocean planet, conquering any who dare stand before you. Or perhaps you wish to simply ply trade through waters infested with other pirates. The choice is yours!

## Each Ship Counts

Ships are persistent across battles, so any ship you lose, you lose forever! You can also capture ships during battles, building your fleet one ship at a time!

## Build an Empire

A reputation system means that traders will remember your name and you get better prices selling to trading posts you already know. Leveling systems mean that every battle is challenge, and as time goes on you can buy even better components for your ships, and components get cheaper as newer, better components are released.
